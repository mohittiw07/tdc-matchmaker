## **Working Session Exercise - "Build the Matchmaker Algo and Dashboard MVP"** 

## **Goal** 

Design and build an internal tool MVP - a simple Matchmaker Dashboard and algo that helps the TDC team manage customers, view profiles, and assign matches. This is a real use case we’re scaling and your work here could directly impact the product roadmap. 

## **Problem Statement** 

TDC matchmakers manage a growing list of clients. Each client is in a different stage of their matchmaking journey, and matchmakers need an easy way to: 

1. View verified profiles 

2. Track where each customer is in the journey 

3. Assign potential matches 

4. Record quick notes from meetings or calls 

Please do your research on dating and matrimonials app before starting the project.
